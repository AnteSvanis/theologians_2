Meet the Theologians

N.T. Wright

Also known as Tom Wright, he’s written over 80 books and even played guitar in a rock band in university. He’s often called the “C.S. Lewis of our time.”

×

Michael Bird

This Aussie theologian used to be a military paratrooper! He mixes serious theology with humor, and even co-authored a book with N.T. Wright.

×

John Piper

Before preaching, Piper wanted to be a doctor—until pneumonia shifted his path to theology. He’s known for “Christian Hedonism” and his quote: “God is most glorified in us when we are most satisfied in Him.”
